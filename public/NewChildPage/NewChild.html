Why do we ask for this information?

Making us aware of your child's allergies can help us keep them safer while in our care, as we can do our best to keep them away from allergens that other children may bring in, such as nuts, eggs, etc. Notifying us of whether or not you will be bringing an EpiPen can help us see whether your child has an EpiPen that we can administer in the event of an allergic reaction. Making us aware of any disabilities that your child may have can help us better accommodate them while in our care, using accommodations that you enter into the designated field.
