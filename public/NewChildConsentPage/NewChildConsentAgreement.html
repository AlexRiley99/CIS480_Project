New Child Consent Agreement

Consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Admin to Obtain Personal Information

I hereby consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Administrators to obtain personal information about my child. I have provided this information of my own free will and I have not been coerced in any way to provide this information. Such information includes:
  • First name
  • Last name
  • Date of birth
I understand what this information will be used for. The person information for myself, other adults, and my child which I have provided, will be used for:
  • Identifying my child
  • Knowing my child's age
And will not be shared with any third parties, per the New Child Disclosure Agreement.

I understand that if I choose not to provide this information, I will not be able to enroll my child in the on-site daycare at Flex and Flow Fitness, as staff will not be able to identify my child.

Consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Admin to Obtain Emergency Contact Information

I hereby consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Administrators to obtain contact information about myself and other adults. I have provided this information of my own free will and I have not been coerced in any way to provide this information. Such contact information includes:
  • First name
  • Last name
  • Phone number
I understand what this information will be used for. The contact information for myself and other adults, which I have provided, will be used for:
  • Contacting me in the event my child experiences an emergency
  • Contacting one of the emergency contacts I have provided in the event my child experiences an emergency and I cannot be reached
And will not be shared with any third parties, per the New Child Disclosure Agreement.

I understand that if I choose not to provide this information, Flex and Flow Fitness staff is not responsible for seeking out emergency contact information, and the default emergency contact if I cannot be reached will be the Emergency Services Department.

Consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Admin to Obtain Medical Information

I hereby consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Administrators to obtain medical information about my child. I have provided this information of my own free will and I have not been coerced in any way to provide this information. Such medical information includes:
  • Known Allergies
  • Whether I will be providing an EpiPen for my child to use
  • Disabilities
  • Accommodations for any disabilities
I understand what this information will be used for. The contact information for myself and other adults, which I have provided, will be used for:
  • Keeping my child safe from allergens that other children may bring in
  • Saving my child's life in the event of an allergic reaction
  • Providing accommodations for any disabilities that my child may have
  • Knowing what accommodations my child needs
And will not be shared with any third parties, per the New Child Disclosure Agreement.

I understand that if I choose not to provide this information, Flex and Flow Fitness staff cannot be held liable for:
  • Any allergic reactions that may occur as a result of staff not being notified of my child's allergy
  • Not using an EpiPen in the event of an allergic reaction as a result of staff not being notified that I provided an Epipen
  • Not accommodating my child's disability as a result of not being notified of my child's disability or any necessary accommodations

Consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Staff to Administer EpiPen

I hereby consent for Flex and Flow Fitness staff to administer an EpiPen in the event that my child has an allergic reaction. I consent to this this of my own free will and I have not been coerced in any way to provide consent.

I understand that I am to provide said EpiPen and notify Flex and Flow Fitness staff that I've provided one.

I understand that if I choose not to provide an EpiPen, Flex and Flow Fitness staff will not be able to take immediate life saving measures in the event that my child experiences an allergic reaction and cannot be held responsible for anything that happens as a result of this.

Consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Daycare Employees to Handle Assistive Technology

I hereby consent for Flex and Flow Fitness daycare staff to handle any assistive technology that my child may need. I consent to this this of my own free will and I have not been coerced in any way to provide consent.
Examples of assistive technology include but are not limited to:
  • Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) devices
  • Hearing aids
  • Wheelchairs
I understand why staff may need to handle my child's assistive technology.
Some reasons include but are not limited to:
  • Helping my child use said assistive technology
I understand that if I choose not to provide this consent, Flex and Flow Fitness staff will not be able to assist my child with their assistive technology, which may result in my child not receiving necessary accommodations for disabilities.

Consent for Flex and Flow Fitness Daycare Employees to Change Diapers and Clothes

I hereby consent for Flex and Flow Fitness daycare staff to change my child's diaper and/or change my child's clothes. I consent to this this of my own free will and I have not been coerced in any way to provide consent.
I understand why staff may need to change my child's diaper or clothes.
Some reasons include:
  • My child has soiled themself and needs a diaper change to, maintain cleanliness, prevent rashes, and prevent urinary tract infections
  • My child has soiled their clothes in some way that warrants changing into a new outfit, to maintain cleanliness
I understand that if I choose not to provide this consent, I will not be able to enroll my child in the on-site daycare at Flex and Flow Fitness, as staff will not be able to clean my child in the event my child soils themself, which can be hazardous to my child's health as well as the health of other children.
