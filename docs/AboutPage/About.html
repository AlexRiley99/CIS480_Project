FAQ

How do I get a membership?

You can obtain a membership in three ways:
  1. You can click on the "Plans" button at the top of your screen. From there, you may select a membership plan. When you click "Select Plan", you will be taken to the member sign up page. Then, you can fill out and submit the form.
  2. You can click on the "Log In" button at the top of your screen. From there, you can click the link to create an account. This will take you to the member sign up page, where you can fill out and submit the form.
  3. You can physically go to a Flex & Flow Fitness location and a staff member will assist you in signing up.
